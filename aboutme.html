About Me

My name is Peter Gutenko, and I am a student at the University of Colorado Boulder. I usually code in Java and C++, and use OpenGL for graphics. I am comfortable with all three major OSes, many common development tools, and design software such as Blender and Photoshop.

I like to make things. Check out my projects and experiments with OpenGL. The project I am currently most proud of would have to be Prompt, my game on Steam.
